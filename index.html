Suomin Aikido Academy (SAA) is aimed to increase comprehension of Aikido practice and reveal its existential values to all people. We are thriving to pave our way through the ignorance-born misconceptions and delusions about this incredible instrument of self-development to the hearts of those people who are ready to be awakened… >>

SAA methodology is designed to nurture and realize the Human Intelligence potential, raising so called “Aiki Mind” within the shell of physical actions. We are here to explain how to approach Aikido to reveal the highest value of the practice. In no way do we promote vague beliefs or teach diluted techniques. Our guidance stands on the ground of intelligence, skill and perseverance. Approach first things first and see how practical the offered methodology is… >>
